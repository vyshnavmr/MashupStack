New books

  • How To Win Friends And Influence People
    -Dale Carnegie

    • How to Win Friends and Influence People written by Dale Carnegie, is a timeless self-help classic that teaches practical principles for effective communication and relationship-building. The book emphasizes understanding others, showing genuine appreciation, and avoiding criticism to foster goodwill. It outlines key strategies like becoming genuinely interested in others, remembering names, and being a good listener to make lasting positive impressions.

  • Million Dollar Weekend
    - Noah Kagan

    • Million Dollar Weekend written by Noah Kagan is a practical and motivating guide for aspiring entrepreneurs who want to launch a successful business quickly. The book emphasizes the importance of validating your business idea within 48 hours by securing paying customers before investing time or money on product development. Kagan encourages focusing on creating "painkiller" products that solve urgent problems rather than "vitamins," which are nice but optional.

  • Do Epic Shit
    - Ankur Warikoo

    • Do Epic Shit written by Ankur Warikoo is a motivational and self-help book encouraging readers to take bold actions and pursue their passions fearlessly. Drawing from his personal experiences as an entrepreneur, Warikoo shares insights about overcoming procrastination, embracing failure as a stepping stone, and cultivating a resilient and growth-oriented mindset. The book emphasizes the importance of self-awareness, continuous learning, and building meaningful connections.
